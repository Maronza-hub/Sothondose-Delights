Our Story

Founded in 2015, Sothondose Delights began as a small home bakery with a passion for creating delicious, handcrafted desserts. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a beloved local bakery known for its quality ingredients and creative flavors.

Today, we continue to bake everything from scratch using traditional methods combined with innovative techniques. Each item is made with care, ensuring every bite is as memorable as the last.
